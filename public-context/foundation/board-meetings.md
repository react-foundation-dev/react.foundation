# React Foundation Board Meetings

> **For Chatbot:** This document covers the React Foundation Governing Board meetings, decisions, and governance.

## Inaugural Board Meeting — March 20, 2026

The first meeting of the React Foundation Governing Board was held on March 20, 2026 to establish operational readiness, approve initial governance and budget decisions, and align on priorities for the Foundation's first year.

### Key Decisions

- Approved preliminary Year 1 budget (iterative, not fixed)
- Agreed to year-end public summary (no immediate budget publication)
- Appointed Seth Webster as Executive Director (unanimous)
- Established meeting cadence: every two months (6/year)
- Selected Discord as primary communication platform
- Confirmed ReactConf will take place in 2026

### Year 1 Budget

The Board approved a preliminary Year 1 budget to enable operations, with the understanding it will be revisited and refined throughout the year. Key discussion points included:

- Alignment between priorities and budget allocation
- Supporting community projects and maintainers
- CI/CD infrastructure costs and sustainability
- Event strategy including ReactConf and community events
- Meta will cover CI/CD infrastructure costs for 2026
- A structured approach to community funding will be developed
- Budget transparency: year-end summary report rather than immediate publication

### Executive Director

Seth Webster was unanimously appointed as Executive Director of the React Foundation.

### Governance

- Board will meet every two months (six times per year)
- An in-person Board meeting is planned
- Discord is the primary communication channel with a private Board channel

### Program Updates

- Community Advisory Board formation is underway
- Asset transition: ongoing migration of GitHub repos, trademarks, domains, and accounts from Meta to the Foundation
- React Native: transition to GitHub-first development model planned within the year
- ReactConf planning has begun

### Engineering Contributions

Each member organization was asked to evaluate contributing approximately 2-3 engineers (full-time or partial) to support React and React Native under the Foundation.

### Technical Governance

Technical governance is being developed as a separate structure from the Governing Board. The technical leadership group has met twice and a representative will participate in future Board meetings.
